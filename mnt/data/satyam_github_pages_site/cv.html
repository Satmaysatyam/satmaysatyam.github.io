💡 Why an Anti-CV?

A standard curriculum vitae is a highly curated timeline of successes, but it leaves out the invisible labor, near-misses, and tough choices that genuinely shape a researcher. In competitive fields like ecology and evolutionary biology, high-stakes selection processes are the norm, and rejection is an essential mechanism of growth. This section maps out the competitive shortlists, funding gaps, and deliberate pivots that occurred behind the scenes—offering a transparent look at the resilience and alternative paths that ultimately built my academic journey.
