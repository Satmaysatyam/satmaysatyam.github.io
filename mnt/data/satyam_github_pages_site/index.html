Behavioral Ecologist • Bioacoustician • Conservation Researcher

Satyam Gupta

Welcome! I am a behavioral ecologist from India, driven by a deep fascination with how animals navigate the complexities of collective life. My research bridges sociality, animal communication, and movement ecology to understand how groups coordinate, make decisions, and thrive in dynamic environments. Currently, I am a DAAD Fellow and PhD researcher at the Deutsches Primatenzentrum (DPZ) - Leibniz Institute for Primate Research in Göttingen, Germany. My doctoral research shifts focus to primates, where I investigate the fundamental drivers and mechanisms of collective movement and multimodal communication in ring-tailed lemurs (Lemur catta) at Affenwald Park, Germany.
